Aurora's Scientific and Technological Institute

Aurora's Scientific and Technological Institute (ASTI) was established in the year 2003 with the name "Church Institute of Technology" as a constituent college of Aurora Consortium under the patronage of Church Educational Society — making it one of the best engineering institutes of Telangana and the oldest among many engineering colleges of United Andhra Pradesh.

ASTI is approved by AICTE, affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH), and is committed to producing globally competent engineers and technology leaders through quality education, research, and industry collaboration.
